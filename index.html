Welcome to LabPPTR


The Laboratory for Plasma Physics and Technologies Research Laboratory (LabPPTR) is a research group based at the Eskisehir Osmangazi University.

Researchers at LabPPTR are primarily engaged with the coating of materials for industrial applications.
